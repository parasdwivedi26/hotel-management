## Problem Statement
Many small and medium hotels manage their daily operations manually, which often leads to
errors such as double room bookings, incorrect billing, and misplaced customer information.
To eliminate these issues, a computerized Hotel Management System is needed to automate
the booking, billing, and food ordering processes.

## Scope of the Project
The system performs the following:
- Customer registration and data collection
- Room booking with type and days of stay
- Food ordering from an in-built restaurant menu
- Calculation of room rent, food charges, and total bill
- Prevents double booking of rooms
- Shows list of booked rooms

## Target Users
- Hotel customers
- Front desk staff
- Small motel/hotel managers

## High-Level Features
- Customer details management
- Room type selection & rent calculation
- Restaurant ordering system
- Total bill generation
- Input validation & error prevention
